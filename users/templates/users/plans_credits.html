Understanding Our Tier System

Our tier system categorizes gyms based on quality, amenities, and facilities. Each tier represents a different level of gym experience, from budget-friendly basic gyms (Tier 1) to premium luxury fitness centers (Tier 4). The higher the tier, the more credits required per visit.

What to Expect at Each Tier

Each tier offers progressively better facilities, equipment, and services. Choose based on your fitness goals and preferences.

T1

Tier 1 - Basic Gyms

5 credits per visit

  • Basic cardio and weight equipment
  • Essential facilities (changing room, basic washroom)
  • No frills, budget-friendly option
  • Limited amenities (usually no AC)
T2

Tier 2 - Standard Gyms

9 credits per visit

  • Modern cardio and strength training equipment
  • Air conditioning available
  • Good changing rooms and clean washrooms
  • Music system and motivating atmosphere
T3

Tier 3 - Premium Gyms

13 credits per visit

  • Top-tier equipment from leading brands
  • Full climate control (AC + heating)
  • Premium changing rooms with lockers
  • Group classes and personal training options
  • Premium sound system and entertainment
T4

Tier 4 - Elite Gyms

18 credits per visit

  • State-of-the-art equipment and technology
  • Luxury amenities (sauna, steam room, spa)
  • Premium changing rooms with premium toiletries
  • Expert trainers and specialized classes
  • Juice bar, supplements, and nutrition guidance
  • Concierge service and towel service
